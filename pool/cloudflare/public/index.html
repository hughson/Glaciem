GROWTH FUND

loading…
While the growth fund is active, every pool-found block pays out an extra bonus on top of the standard chain reward, distributed proportionally by share contribution. The bonus comes from a pre-funded balance, not from anyone else's mining. Once the fund drains, payouts return to normal block reward.

PAYOUT MATURITY

loading…
Block rewards are locked for 60 confirmations before the pool can spend them — same rule as Monero. Each pool-found block matures about two hours after it's mined. Payouts only fire from unlocked balance, so a pending credit means your share is sitting in a maturing coinbase output and will be paid as soon as it unlocks.

HOW TO CONNECT

In the Glaciem Miner (v1.1.16 recommended, all four platforms supported):

Settings → Mining mode: POOL
Pool URL:            https://glaciem-pool.frostmine.workers.dev
Payout address:      your wallet (auto-filled from your embedded wallet)

Or use any other Glaciem-compatible pool by entering its URL there.
